About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization that was started on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. The organization works to help people in different parts of India through education, food support, women empowerment, environmental protection, animal care, and skill development. Its main goal is to improve the lives of people and build a better and more caring society.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project SEVA

Project SEVA helps poor and needy families by providing food, clothes, and other basic necessities. It aims to support people during difficult times.

Project BachpanShala

This project provides education and learning opportunities for underprivileged children so they can build a better future.

Project Jeev

Project Jeev focuses on feeding, protecting, and caring for stray animals while spreading awareness about animal welfare.

Project Udaan

This project encourages women to become independent by providing skill training and opportunities for personal and financial growth.

Project Prakriti

Project Prakriti promotes tree plantation and environmental awareness to make our surroundings cleaner and greener.

Project Vikas

Project Vikas helps students and young people improve their skills through training, internships, and career development programs.
